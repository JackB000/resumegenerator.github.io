Choose A Resume Format

Chronological

What is a Chronological Resume?

Most common resume format. Focuses on work experience with items listed in order of most recent to oldest.

Functional

What is a Functional Resume?

Skill-based resume format, focuses on skills that are relevant to the role being pursued.

Combination

What is a Combination Resume?

Uses both the Chronological and Functional formats. Places equal emphasis on skills and work experience.
